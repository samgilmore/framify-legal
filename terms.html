Framify Terms of Service

Effective Date: September 2025

1. Subscription

Framify offers three subscription plans: weekly, monthly, and annual. All plans unlock full access to the app. Payments are handled securely through Apple’s App Store infrastructure and Superwall. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will remain active across devices associated with your account.

2. Content and Storage

Framify does not store your photos remotely. All data is saved locally on your device and optionally synced through iCloud. Framify stores two types of records:

  • Photos — metadata, frame settings, and a reference to the original image asset in your photo library.
  • Presets — saved frame style settings tied to your user account.

Framify does not copy or host your original photos. If you delete an image from your device, it will no longer be available inside the app.

You are responsible for maintaining your subscription through the App Store. If your subscription expires, Framify may restrict access until it is renewed.
