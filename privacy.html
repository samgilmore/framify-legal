Framify Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

1. Data Collection

Framify does not collect or store personal data on external servers. All information remains on your device and may be backed up through iCloud if enabled.

2. Stored Records

The app stores only two kinds of records:

  • Photos — references to your image assets and their associated EXIF metadata plus applied frame styles.
  • Presets — your custom frame styling configurations.

3. Third-Party Services

Payments and subscriptions are managed by Apple’s App Store and Superwall. Framify does not process or store payment information.

4. Your Control

You can delete the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data. If you delete a photo from your device, Framify will no longer display it.
